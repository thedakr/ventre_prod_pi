## What We're Building

**AI Agents** (3 for MVP) + **Proven Methodology** (from expert entrepreneurs) + **Cohort Facilitation** (human + AI) = Accessible entrepreneurship for underserved populations

**MVP Agents:** Chief of Staff (coordinator), Market Discovery (customer research), Methodology Coach (framework teacher)

## Three Core Hypotheses

1. **Latent entrepreneurs exist** in poor populations (AI + methodology will surface them)
2. **This methodology is uniquely suited** for underserved populations (optimized for low-resource contexts)
3. **AI can transform** entrepreneurship practice (removes barriers: knowledge, network, expertise)

**Solution:** 4-week methodology extraction (Weeks 1-4) feeds agent development (Weeks 3-6).
