# Vibe Entrepreneurship: One-Page Summary

**Mission:** Enable would-be entrepreneurs below poverty line to start/run businesses with AI as mentor, partner, analyst, and employee.

**Target:** ‚â§150% US federal poverty line | **Timeline:** First cohort in 14-16 weeks | **Team:** 3.25 people

---

## What We're Building

**AI Agents** (6 for MVP) + **Proven Methodology** (from expert entrepreneurs) + **Cohort Facilitation** (human + AI) = Accessible entrepreneurship for underserved populations

**MVP Agents:** Chief of Staff, Market Discovery, Methodology Coach, Business/Revenue Model, Route-to-Market, Strategic Conviction

**Delivery:** 4-week cohort, 10-15 participants, virtual sessions, web portal for 24/7 agent access

---

## Three Core Hypotheses

1. **Latent entrepreneurs exist** in poor populations (AI + methodology will surface them)
2. **This methodology is uniquely suited** for underserved populations (optimized for low-resource contexts)
3. **AI can transform** entrepreneurship practice (removes barriers: knowledge, network, expertise)

---

## Critical Insight: Methodology MUST Come First

**Problem:** Can't implement effective agents without first codifying the methodology individuals will engage with.

**Solution:** 3-week methodology extraction (Weeks 1-3) feeds agent development (Weeks 4-8), with proper testing time before launch.
