# Quick Start: Methodology Extraction

**Goal:** Distill the entrepreneurship mastery framework from materials and experts within 3 weeks

**Why:** You can't implement effective AI agents without first codifying the methodology they'll teach.

---

## The Big Picture

You have **3 critical assets**:
1. Materials showing real executions of the methodology
2. Direct access to 2 expert entrepreneurs who created it
3. Agent specifications (but they need the methodology to work properly)

You need to **extract, synthesize, and codify** the methodology into a framework that:
- Defines the phases, frameworks, and principles
- Captures adaptations for underserved populations
- Guides AI agent implementation
- Serves as the foundation for your program

---

## Week 1: Materials Analysis Begins

### Day 1-2: Materials Inventory (HIGHEST PRIORITY)
**Action:** List everything you have
- **Who:** Product Manager/PI + Methodology & Cohort Facilitator + LXD
- **Time:** 4-6 hours
- **Template:** `docs/templates/materials-inventory.md`
- **Output:** Complete list of all methodology materials with priorities

**How to do it:**
1. Open the materials inventory template
2. Go through every folder, drive, file you have
3. List each material with as much detail as you can
4. Prioritize: HIGH (facilitator guides, methodology docs, exercises) ‚Üí MEDIUM ‚Üí LOW

### Day 2-3: Pick Top 3-5 Materials
**Action:** Identify the highest-value materials to analyze first
- **Who:** Methodology & Cohort Facilitator
- **Time:** 1-2 hours
- **Output:** List of 3-5 materials to extract from first

**Prioritize:**
1. Facilitator guides (show structure and flow)
2. Methodology documentation (explicit frameworks)
3. Core participant exercises (reveal approach)
4. Case studies (proof points)

### Day 3-5: Analyze Top 3-5 Materials
**Action:** Deep extraction from high-priority materials
- **Who:** Methodology & Cohort Facilitator (lead) + LXD (support)
- **Time:** 10-12 hours (2-3 hours per material)
- **Template:** `docs/templates/material-extraction.md`
- **Output:** Extraction document for each material

**For each material:**
1. First pass: Skim to understand purpose and key concepts (15-30 min)
2. Second pass: Extract frameworks, principles, exercises, sequence (30-60 min)
3. Document using extraction template
4. Note questions for experts

### Day 4-5: Prepare for Expert Session 1
**Action:** Draft agenda based on initial material analysis
- **Who:** Methodology & Cohort Facilitator (draft) + Product Manager/PI (review)
- **Time:** 2-3 hours
- **Template:** `docs/templates/expert-session-1-agenda.md`
- **Output:** Session 1 agenda with targeted questions

**Include:**
- What you think you learned from materials
- What's unclear or missing
- Specific questions about methodology structure and philosophy

### End of Week 1: Schedule Expert Sessions
**Action:** Book time with methodology experts
- **Who:** Product Manager/PI
- **Time:** 30 minutes
- **Output:** 3 sessions scheduled (90 min each)

**Sessions to schedule:**
- Session 1: Methodology Overview & Validation (Week 2)
- Session 2: Decision-Making & Facilitation Wisdom (Week 2-3)
- Session 3: Underserved Population Adaptations (Week 3)

## Week 2: Expert Engagement + Continued Analysis

### Day 1: Expert Session 1
**Action:** Conduct methodology overview and validation session
- **Who:** Methodology & Cohort Facilitator (lead) + You (attend)
- **Time:** 90 minutes + 2-3 hours synthesis
- **Agenda:** `docs/templates/expert-session-1-agenda.md`
- **Output:** Session recording + synthesis document

**Key topics:**
- Methodology overview (phases, structure, philosophy)
- Validate what you learned from materials
- Learn what's unique to this methodology
- Understand adaptations for underserved populations

**CRITICAL:** Synthesize notes within 24 hours while fresh!

### Day 2-4: Continue Material Analysis
**Action:** Analyze next 3-5 high-priority materials
- **Who:** Methodology & Cohort Facilitator
- **Time:** 10-12 hours
- **Output:** Additional extraction documents

**Now that you have expert context:**
- Materials will make more sense
- You'll see patterns more clearly
- You can validate interpretations against expert insights

### Day 5: Pattern Synthesis
**Action:** Identify themes across all materials analyzed so far
- **Who:** Methodology & Cohort Facilitator
- **Time:** 4-6 hours
- **Output:** Provisional methodology synthesis document

**Questions to answer:**
- What frameworks appear repeatedly?
- What principles are emphasized consistently?
- What's the meta-structure (phases/stages)?
- What's unique to this methodology?
- What's specifically adapted for underserved populations?
- Where are there gaps or contradictions?

### End of Week 2: Prepare for Expert Sessions 2-3
**Action:** Draft agendas for remaining expert sessions
- **Who:** Methodology & Cohort Facilitator
- **Time:** 2 hours
- **Output:** Session 2-3 agendas with targeted questions

- **Time:** 60-90 minutes
- **Output:** Validated methodology framework v1.0

3. Get corrections, additions, clarifications
4. Incorporate feedback

**Action:** Translate methodology framework into agent-specific guidance
- **Who:** Methodology & Cohort Facilitator (content) + AI Engineer (feasibility review)
- **Time:** 4-6 hours per agent (MVP = 6 agents = 24-36 hours)
- **Output:** Implementation spec for each MVP agent

**For each agent:**
- How this agent supports the methodology
- What frameworks it uses
- What principles it embodies
- Language and tone guidelines
- Example interactions
- Handoff criteria to human facilitator
- Adaptations for underserved populations

## Critical Success Factors

### 1. Methodology & Cohort Facilitator is the Lead
**Why:** Pedagogy expertise, understands learning design
**Time required:** 40-50% for Weeks 1-3 (15-20 hours/week)
**If not feasible:** Adjust timeline or bring in support

### 2. Materials Analysis Must Happen First
**Why:** You need context before expert sessions
**Don't skip:** Even quick analysis is better than none
**Temptation:** "Let's just ask experts everything" ‚Üí You'll waste their time and miss details

### 3. Synthesize Expert Sessions Immediately
**Why:** Memory fades fast, insights are clearer when fresh
**Rule:** Within 24 hours of each session
**Tip:** Record sessions (with permission) for reference

### 4. Focus on What's Unique
**Why:** Generic startup advice is everywhere
**Dig for:** What makes THIS methodology different
**Especially:** Adaptations for underserved populations

### 5. Parallel Track with Agent Development
**Why:** Efficient use of team time across 14-16 week timeline
**Approach:**
- Weeks 1-3: Methodology extraction (this document's focus)
- Weeks 4-8: AI Engineer implements 6 agents + portal with methodology
- Weeks 9-12: Refine agents based on methodology fidelity testing
- Weeks 13-16: Cohort launch and real-world validation
